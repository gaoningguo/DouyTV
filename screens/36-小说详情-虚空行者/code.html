Synopsis

In the sprawling mega-city of Neo-Veridia, where the elite live in sky-piercing arcologies and the rest scavenge in the neon-drenched lower levels, Jax is a 'Void Runner'. He navigates the perilous digital gaps between corporate mainframes, stealing data for the highest bidder. When a routine extraction goes catastrophically wrong, Jax uncovers a conspiracy that threatens to plunge the entire city into chaos. Now, hunted by cybernetically enhanced corporate enforcers and betrayed by his own syndicate, he must race against time to expose the truth before the city's power grid—and millions of lives—are permanently shut down.
